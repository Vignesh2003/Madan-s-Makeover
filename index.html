About Madan's Makeover Luxury Salon Welcome to Madan’s Makeover (MMS) Luxury Salon, where passion meets perfection and luxury becomes a way of life. Founded by Madan, a stylist with over 22 years of experience, MMS is a sanctuary of elegance designed to celebrate individuality and elevate grooming to an art form. What began as a heartfelt pursuit of passion has blossomed into a space where quality, creativity, and personalized care come together to create unforgettable experiences. At MMS, we believe that every client deserves not just exceptional service but also a lasting bond with their stylist. Here, luxury is not just a promise—it’s a feeling.
